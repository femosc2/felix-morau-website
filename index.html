An information architecture students personal website!


I'm a 22 year old student who studies Information Architecture at Malmö University.

I grew up in Helsingborg, Sweden where I attended Husensjö Skolan (Years 1-5), Kunskapsskolan (Years 6-9) and attended the economics program at Nicolaiskolan (Years 9-12). Afterwards I started studying Marketing at Linnaeus University but found out that I did not enjoy economics or business management. Instead I took a year off where I took some courses in various subjects. I enrolled in Malmö University September 2017.

I've been enjoying both web design and programming since taking the courses and made this website as a form to track all my projects, social media and GitHub while also practicing my web design skills.

Make sure to follow my social media and do not hesitate to contact me if you have any questions about my projects, information architecture or Malmö University.
